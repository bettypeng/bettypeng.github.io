PROJECTS

First Aide

A project under Systers during the Grace Hopper Open Source Code Day.

First Aide is an open source app created for Peace Corps volunteers, containing information, resources, and services pertaining to sexual assault. We decided to implement it as a hybrid mobile app powered by Angular and Ionic. I contributed to the front end and helped organize content.

WeatherMix

A solution for all nature-lovers confined to the indoors: Experience the beauty and ambient sounds of nature for study, meditation, or sleep, based on local weather conditions and forecast at your current location.

Built using HTML/CSS, jQuery, and the Wunderground API.

Wikipodia

Quick search engine for easy scanning of Wikipedia entries.

Fun experiment with the MediaWiki API.

Pacman

The classic Pacman game, written in Java.

Learned how to apply a breadth-first search algorithm.

Drunk Portraits

A collection of self-portraits and interviews with inebriated Brown students.

After a few drinks, there is a shift in the way we navigate the world, in the way we view our past and tell our stories, and in the way we perceive ourselves. This project is all about capturing the movements, visions, and perspectives students have while under the influence.

Betty's Anatomy

Digital non-fiction.

A collection of stories based on body parts.
