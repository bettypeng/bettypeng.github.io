Hey there! I'm Betty, a coffee-drinker and junior studying Computer Science and English at Brown University. I love front-end development, computer graphics, and design. My current work mainly centers on human-computer interaction with Brown's Pen and Touch Computing Research group, where we are building a collaborative workspace application that opens up a world of possibilities in on-screen pen and touch gestures.

In all my work, I'm driven by one thing: making our world a cooler and more beautiful place.

In my spare time, I like to practice one-armed handstands. Let's build great things together.
